Album released with Centaur Records: Polychrome

We released an album of original works for solo flute and solo clarinet in January 2020 on Centaur Records, one of the oldest and largest independent classical music labels in the US. The music was composed in Spring 2018, recorded in May 2018, and mixed and mastered in Summer 2018.
